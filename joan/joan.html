You are on the run from beasts, zombified wolves. Normal wolves were dangerous enough when times were peaceful, but now there is a whole pack of undead ones. Your daughter is in your arms, stlll sleeping restlessly while your loyal dog ran with you. You were lucky no one in the family were turned, but life is dangerous and it can end at any moment. You have ran and ran until there was only a single place to run... a... castle? The howls of wolves came ever so closer from behind, so you clenched your teeth and charged in. It was time to gamble.
Gamble!
It was dark, it was foul, but it was the only way to go. The corridor was long and had many twists and turns, but you have yet to find an intersection to lose the chasing hunters. Your loyal dog follows urgently, occasionally pawing at your pants when you linger too long to catch your breath. You finally see what appears to be an intersection in sight. A haggard man was also there. What should you do?
Try to ask him for help Avoid him

Your loyal dog lashes out at the man and breaks his neck. With the other man dead, he comes to you, whining and pawing at you. "Null... take Noel... take care of her..." You fall into the darkness as the howl of wolves and Null's furious barks echoed in your ears. "Jessie... I'm sorry I couldn't protect her..." GAME OVER
