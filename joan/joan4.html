Survival of All Kinds

Path: Joan

Game Over

You went past the left turn and kept going straight.

But to your utter despair, you discovered too late it was a zombie nest.

You try to run back out but the wolves were already past the other corridor from which you could have ran into.

With Noel in one arm, you brandish your knife while Null did his best to protect you two.

You would kill a zombie but then a zombified wolf would attack you from another place.

You quickly tired but still fought desperately.

"Not Noel, just not her. I promised her, I promised Jessie. Please, Jessie, bless us."

"Whimper."

"Null!"

That one distraction was the end to all. Null laid bleeding out on the ground and you were mauled, falling to the ground on your back.

"Dad?"

That sleepy voice was the last thing you heard.
