End

Noel and you decided to keep going straight. This seems to be the path to freedom.

You come across a small bag of food and water next to a corpse and take it to Noel.

There was another path but you two could clearly see it was a dead end with even more corpses.

Thankfully it has nothing else.

Soon enough, you two could both see the entrance. Both of you happily run over. Even if you two couldn't survive out there, it was better than staying inside.

Besides, they have each other.

That's all that matters right now.
