Survival of All Kinds

Path: Noel

End

You and Null carefully peeked over the corner, and what you saw made tears fall.

It was your dad, but zombie-fied.

Null quickly advances and kills him before he could harm you.

After the zombie falls and no longer moved, you crouched by it and cried, weeping for the remaining family member that was now lost to you as well.

You and Null left after you dried your tears. Though you may not have any more family left, you will do your best to survive with Null. He was also family. Although a slightly different one.

You will do your best to survive.

After all, your dad left you a message:

Noel, live strongly and freely. Dad loves you.
