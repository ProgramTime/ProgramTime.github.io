Survival of All Kinds

Path: Noel

End

The route is long and hard, you feel yourself steadily tiring. But you persisted and avoided the strange corners and kept on walking.

Tightly clutching your bunny, you stumble upon a half-eaten corpse with a bag, you take the bag and continue onwards.

Eventually, you find the entrance. Even though you walk out of it, you feel empty.

There is no one with you, and you don't know if you will ever find your dad or Null.

You collapsed outside and cried.

After a while, you stood up and clutched your last personal belonging and brave yourself against the lonesome world.
