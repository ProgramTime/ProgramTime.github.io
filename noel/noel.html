You are a lost girl, the last thing you remember is sleeping with your dad smiling at you from above and Null snuggling into your side. Your dad is the only parent you have since your mom died the day you were born. Your dad got you Null when you were young because he couldn't always be there when he had to work. But where were they now? And where were you?
Walk On!
There are two paths before you, scared but determined, you make your choice. Shall it be the left or the right?
Left Right
There were some corpses of wolves lying around, their red blood making small puddles you stepped over carefully. You take the left and meet another intersection. One goes left while the other goes straight ahead. You can hear something from the right, it sounds rather like scratching. Should you risk it?
Straight Right
Oh no! What terrible luck! Your first choice and it's a zombie nest! A child with nothing but the dress on her body and a bunny plushie can do nothing in front of those hungry monsters. You can only close your eyes and cry as their claws tears into you and their teeth rips you apart. GAME OVER

Even though you walk out of it, you feel empty. There is no one with you, and you don't know if you will ever find your dad or Null. You collapse outside and cry. After a while, you stand up and clutch your last personal belonging and brave yourself against the lonesome world. END
